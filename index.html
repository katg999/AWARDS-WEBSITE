Elevate Bridge Builders Announces Shortlisted Candidates for 2025 Awards

Recognizing outstanding contributions to sanitation and hygiene across the region

Dubai, UAE - From school corridors to construction sites, shopping malls to smart toilets, a quiet revolution in hygiene is taking shape across the Middle East and Africa. Today, that progress takes center stage as we announce the shortlisted candidates for the 2025 Sanitation and Hygiene Excellence Awards.

The organizing committee is proud to reveal this year's shortlisted organizations, schools, innovators, and individuals who have demonstrated exceptional commitment to promoting cleanliness, protecting public health, and ensuring dignity through better sanitation practices.

With over 500 nominations received from 22 countries across the Middle East and Africa, the awards showcase impactful initiatives ranging from digital handwashing education programs to solar-powered sanitation solutions and nationwide hygiene awareness campaigns reaching millions.

"Good hygiene is not just about soap and water - it is about education, dignity, sustainability, and care. This year's finalists reflect the growing leadership across our region in turning hygiene into a cultural priority, powered by innovative thinking and community commitment."

Nabakka Sandra, CEO and Co-founder of Elevate Bridge Builders UK Ltd
