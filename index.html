    Entry Process & Criteria

  • Manufacturers and distributors of cleaning and hygiene equipment, chemicals, janitorial tools and software are eligible to enter the awards in the applicable categories.Service providers who may themselves include Cleaning Contractors, Building Services Contractors, Housekeeping Companies, and Facilities Management companies involved in Soft FM.

  • The End User can be a public or private facility/building maintained by an in-house team or outsourced to a service provider. These can include and are not limited to Hotels, Malls, Airports, Banks, Corporate/Government Buildings, Restaurants, Hospitals, Schools, Universities, etc.

  • Each company/facility must be at least 1 year old in operations in the MEA region.

  • Individuals being nominated must have a valid visa for a Middle Eastern country.

  • Applications are open to companies based in the United Arab Emirates. Separate categories have been opened for companies in other countries of MEA.
